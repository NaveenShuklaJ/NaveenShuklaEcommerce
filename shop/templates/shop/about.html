Discover Our Journey!

Welcome to Our Nav-Ecommerce Pvt Ltd., a leading electronic commerce enterprise headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded by Naveen Shukla in 2021, we started our journey with a primary focus on sales and later diversified into various product categories, including consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products.

With a mission to provide exceptional service, we compete vigorously with key players such as Amazon's Indian subsidiary and our domestic rival Snapdeal. As of March 2017, we proudly held a significant 39.5% market share in India's booming e-commerce industry. Our expertise extends to dominating the apparel sector, solidified by strategic acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com. In the realm of electronics and mobile phones, we stand 'neck and neck' with global giant Amazon. Additionally, we are proud owners of PhonePe, a mobile payments service built on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).

Our journey took a remarkable turn in August 2018 when the U.S.-based retail giant Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in Our Company for an impressive US$16 billion, valuing us at an astounding $22 billion.
